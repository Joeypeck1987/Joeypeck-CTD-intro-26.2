About

I am an entry-level web developer and aspiring data engineer based in Lubbock, Texas. I build projects with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, APIs, GitHub, and responsive layouts. I recently completed Code the Dream’s Intro to JavaScript program, where I practiced DOM manipulation, form events, asynchronous fetch requests, GitHub workflows, and API-based projects. My background in food service and warehouse work has helped me build reliability, attention to detail, consistency, and the ability to work under pressure.

“A page can be plain and still carry weather. A skill can begin clumsy and still become a craft.”
What I am building toward

A life where technical skill and creative nerve are not separate rooms. I want to understand systems, make useful things, and keep the work alive enough to avoid becoming mechanical.
